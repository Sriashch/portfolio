Beyond the portfolio

I'm curious about why things work — products, people, the small decisions that make an interface feel obvious instead of confusing. That curiosity is what pulled me from writing code into caring about the person on the other side of the screen.

Most of what I've learned came from building something, breaking it, and figuring out why. Photo a Week started as a weekend idea and turned into a real app with accounts and a database, mostly because I kept asking "but what if someone actually used this?"

I like clarity. I like when things make sense. And I like shipping.
